About Us

Growing with our community since 1985

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Adam Hall, our garden center started as a small family business with a passion for plants and gardening. Over the years, we've grown into one of the most trusted garden centers in the region, serving generations of gardening enthusiasts.

Our commitment to quality, customer service, and sustainable practices has helped us build lasting relationships with our community. We take pride in offering expert advice and the highest quality plants and products.

Our Mission

At Adam Halls Garden Center, our mission is to help people create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that bring joy and connection to nature. We believe in:

  • Providing high-quality plants and products
  • Offering expert gardening advice and education
  • Supporting sustainable gardening practices
  • Building strong community relationships
